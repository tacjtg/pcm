About the Instructor

Levi Montgomery

With over 35 years in law enforcement — from SWAT and undercover operations to forensic consultation — Chief Instructor Montgomery brings an extensive background in police tactics, weapons use, and martial arts to every training session.

His unique methods have been recognized and implemented by special operation groups including Team MIKE in Kosovo and H8 Teams in Iraq. His non-conventional approach constantly challenges current methods to develop better solutions.
